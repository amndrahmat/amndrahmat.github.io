My Project SMASH ENERGY

This IoT project is about Trash Bins that can sort waste into two types, metal waste, and non-metal waste. This trash Bin is equipped with solar energy (Solar Panel) so, it does not require additional electrical energy. This project is equipped with an Android application and a Web application as a means of information and notification when the Trash Bins is full.
